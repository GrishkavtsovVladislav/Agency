Current directions:
Turkey

Turkey

Turkey is famous for its centuries-old historical monuments, beautiful beaches, excellent cuisine and hospitable people. In Turkey, you can visit many museums, palaces, mosques and other attractions, such as the monument to Istanbul - the Blue Mosque, Topkapi Palace, the Museum of Anatolian Civilization and much more. In addition, Turkey is a famous resort where you can enjoy the warm sea and sandy beaches, as well as taste the local cuisine, which is famous for its delicious dishes such as kebabs, meze and baklava. In general, Turkey is a great place to relax and travel.

Egypt

Egypt

Egypt is famous for its ancient pyramids, sphinxes, temples and other archaeological sites that attract many tourists every year. In addition, Egypt is one of the largest countries in Africa and the Middle East, has a developed economy, rich culture and history, as well as beautiful beaches, resorts and marine aquariums that attract lovers of beach holidays and water activities. Egypt is also known for its cuisine, which is a mixture of Arabic, African and Mediterranean cuisines and includes many delicious foods and drinks.

UAE

UAE

The UAE is one of the most developed and richest countries in the world thanks to the oil industry and tourism. Here you can find upscale hotels, luxury shopping centers, modern attractions, as well as enjoy the beaches and exotic nature. The UAE is also known for its cultural and historical landmarks such as mosques, forts and museums. The UAE has a hot climate and Arabic is the official language, although English is widely used in the tourism industry.

Georgia

Georgia

In Georgia, you can find mountain peaks, picturesque valleys, lakes, rivers and sea coasts. Georgia is also known for its monasteries, churches and fortresses, which are part of the country's rich cultural and historical heritage. Georgian cuisine is famous for its national dishes such as khachapuri, khinkali, mtsvadi and others. Georgia is also known for its wines, which are produced in various regions of the country. Georgia has a varied climate, from subtropical on the coast to continental in the mountains. Georgian is the official language, but Russian and English are widely used in the tourism industry.

Italy

Italy

Italy is located on the Apennine Peninsula and is washed by the Tyrrhenian, Ionian and Adriatic seas. Italy is one of the most visited tourist destinations in the world, attracting travelers with its historic cities, beautiful beaches, mountain resorts, luxurious wine regions and gourmet cuisine. Among the main attractions of Italy are the Colosseum, the Vatican, Tuscany, Florence, Rome, Venice, the Amalfi Coast and the island of Sicily.

Spain

Spain

Spain is famous for its beautiful beaches, picturesque towns, rich culture and favorites, and exquisite hunting. Among the main attractions of Spain stands out Barcelona with its famous landmarks built as the Basilica of the Sagrada Familia and Park Güell, Madrid with its museum and palace, Seville with its Arab palaces and Granada with the Alhambra - the famous palace of the Moors. In addition, a collection of its festivals, the founder of both the Pamplona Bullfight and the Tomatina Festival.

Cyprus

Cyprus

Cyprus is famous for its beautiful beaches, warm climate, rich history and culture. The capital of Cyprus is Nicosia, which is the cultural and economic center of the island. Cyprus is also famous for its archaeological finds such as the ancient city of Kourion and the ancient theater in Paphos. In addition, Cyprus is a popular resort attracting tourists with its beautiful beaches, crystal clear waters and water sports facilities. Cyprus is also famous for its cuisine, which combines Greek, Turkish and English influences.

Cuba

Cuba

Cuba is an island nation in the Caribbean, known for its rich history, culture and natural attractions. Here you can find many beautiful beaches, picturesque cities, architectural monuments, museums and national parks. Cuba is also famous for its music, dance and cuisine, which reflect a mix of Spanish, African and Taino cultures. One of the main symbols of Cuba is the cigar, which has been produced here for over a century. Cuba is a popular tourist destination that attracts people from all over the world with its unique flavor and atmosphere.

Bulgaria

Bulgaria

Bulgaria is a state known for its rich history, culture and natural attractions. Here you can find many beautiful cities, picturesque beaches, mountain resorts, architectural monuments, museums and national parks. Bulgaria is famous for its rich cuisine, which includes many local dishes and drinks. One of the main symbols of Bulgaria are roses, which have been produced here for more than a century and are used for the production of perfumes and cosmetics. Bulgaria is a popular tourist destination that attracts people from all over the world with its unique color and atmosphere.

Montenegro

Montenegro

Montenegro is a state known for its beautiful nature, mountain landscapes and picturesque beaches on the Adriatic coast. Here you can find many beautiful towns and villages, architectural monuments, museums and national parks. Montenegro is famous for its rich cuisine, which includes many local dishes and drinks, as well as traditional cheese and wine production. One of the main symbols of Montenegro is Mount Lovcen, on which the mausoleum and museum of the outstanding Montenegrin poet and statesman Peter II Negosh is located. Montenegro is a popular tourist destination that attracts people from all over the world with its unique flavor and atmosphere.

India

India

India is the largest country known for its vibrant cultural traditions, rich historical heritage, varied cuisine and colorful festivals. In India, you can find everything - from huge metropolitan areas with tall skyscrapers to quiet villages, from ancient temples to modern technology. India is also famous for its natural beauty, including the Himalayas, the Thar Desert, rainforests and ocean shores. However, India also faces challenges such as inequality, poverty and pollution.

Dominican

Dominican

The Dominican Republic is a beautiful country in the Caribbean known for its white sand beaches, turquoise waters and abundance of palm trees. Here you can enjoy the warm climate all year round, taste the local cuisine, including the famous seafood, and get to know the unique culture and traditions of the Dominicans. The country is also famous for its natural attractions such as national parks, waterfalls and mountains. The Dominican Republic has many opportunities for outdoor activities such as snorkeling, diving, surfing and golf.
